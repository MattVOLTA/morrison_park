# St. John's Regional M&A Target Scan

**Date:** 2026-02-27
**Requested by:** Ken Skinner (Jan 12, 2026 email)
**Scope:** Greater St. John's metro (St. John's, Mount Pearl, Paradise, CBS, Torbay) + select provincial companies
**Method:** 6 parallel research agents covering manufacturing, construction, food/healthcare/distribution, oil & gas/marine, tech/services, and M&A signals

---

## Executive Summary

Intensive search identified **40+ privately held mid-market companies** in the St. John's metro area across MPA's priority sectors. The region is experiencing an active M&A cycle — **7 notable transactions closed in 2024-2026** (Pennecon $708M, Grieg Seafood $1.3B global, Genoa Design, CORE Engineering, Crosbie Job Insurance, Central Dairies, SaltWire). This validates strong buyer appetite for NL assets.

The remaining pool of independent private companies is *shrinking*, making this an ideal time for Ken to engage. Key themes:

1. **Succession wave is real** — Many companies founded in the 1970s-1990s by tradespeople/entrepreneurs now 60-80+ years old
2. **Recent founder deaths** — Roger Pike (A. Harvey/Pike Group, Oct 2023), William Bursey (Bursey Mfg, July 2023), Jim Weir Sr. (Weir's Construction, Dec 2024), Harry Steele (Steele Hotels, Jan 2022)
3. **Non-family CEO installations** — Crosbie Group (Jan 2022), Penney Group (2021) — classic pre-transaction signals
4. **Hall of Fame inductions** — Frank Howard (Capital Auto, 2025), Ruth Roberts-Tetford (Dorman Roberts, 2025) — late-career recognition pattern
5. **Island premium** — Geographic isolation creates natural barriers to entry, making incumbents strategically valuable to national platforms

**Already in DB (skip):** Cal LeGrow Insurance, CoLab Software, Mysa Smart Thermostats, Sparrow BioAcoustics

**Already sold (off-market):** Pennecon (GIP, June 2025), Genoa Design (Membertou/Horizon, Jan 2026), CORE Engineering (BPA, Jan 2026), Crosbie Job Insurance (BrokerLink, June 2025), DF Barnes (JV Driver), Newdock (Indigenous consortium, 2024), Grieg Seafood NL (Cermaq/Mitsubishi, July 2025)
