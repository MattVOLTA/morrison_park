## Company Overview

Morrison Park Advisors (MPA) was founded in November 2006 by experienced investment banking professionals from larger bank-owned platforms. The firm has evolved into a prominent independent investment bank with offices in Toronto and Calgary.

**Regulatory credentials:**
- Exempt Market Dealer (Ontario, Alberta, British Columbia)
- US activities through Marco Polo Securities Inc. (SEC-registered)
- IMAP membership (60+ offices, 40+ countries)
- Strategic alliance with DNA Capital

## Triggering Events

Based on deal history, common triggers for MPA engagement:

1. **Founder succession** - Owner ready to exit
2. **PE exit** - Fund life ending, need liquidity
3. **Strategic review** - Board exploring options
4. **Market consolidation** - Industry M&A activity
5. **Capital needs** - Growth, refinancing, restructuring
6. **Regulatory pressure** - Forcing strategic decisions
7. **Contested situations** - Need independent advisor

## Key Observations for Atlantic Canada Strategy

1. **No visible Atlantic Canada deals** in public track record - opportunity or gap?

2. **Sector strengths align** with regional economy:
   - Energy (NL offshore, renewables)
   - Mining (NS, NB operations)
   - Healthcare (regional systems)
   - Financial services (credit unions)

3. **Sector gaps** vs. Atlantic economy:
   - Seafood/aquaculture - no MPA track record
   - Tourism/hospitality - limited
   - Agriculture/food processing - limited

4. **Deal size sweet spot** ($30M-$150M) matches Atlantic Canada mid-market

5. **Special committee expertise** valuable for public companies in region

6. **Mission-driven experience** relevant for credit unions, healthcare, housing
