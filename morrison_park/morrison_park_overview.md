 Morrison Park Advisors - Detailed Research Report

  Company Overview

  Morrison Park Advisors (MPA) is an independent, partner-owned investment banking advisory firm headquartered in Toronto, Canada. Founded in November 2006 by a group of former Scotia Capital investment bankers, the firm has grown to approximately 30 
  professionals.

  Location: 9 Temperance Street, Third Floor, Toronto, Ontario M5H 1Y6

  The Globe and Mail has described MPA as "continuing its project of recreating Scotia Capital's investment banking department" - confirming what Ken mentioned about the founders being ex-Scotia Capital bankers.

  ---
  Competitive Positioning

  Differentiators:
  1. Independence - Partner-owned, no conflicts from lending/trading arms
  2. Senior-led execution - Partners stay involved throughout deals
  3. Special Committee expertise - Willing to provide opinions where large banks won't due to litigation risk
  4. Mid-market focus - Multi-billion deal experience applied to companies valued in tens of millions
  5. Long-term relationships - Emphasis on trust-building over transactional work

  Challenge (per Ken): Growth constrained by human bandwidth - "nothing happens unless I do it"
