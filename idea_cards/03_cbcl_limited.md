## Special Note: Employee-Owned Transaction Dynamics

Unlike typical founder-owned or family-held companies, CBCL's employee-owned structure creates unique considerations:

1. **Multiple stakeholders** - Any transaction requires buy-in from employee shareholders, not just board/owners
2. **Cultural sensitivity** - Employees chose employee ownership; may resist external acquisition
3. **Valuation complexity** - Internal share pricing may differ from market valuation
4. **Retention risk** - Key employees may leave post-acquisition if culture changes
5. **Advisory positioning** - MPA could add value by navigating employee stakeholder dynamics

This may be better suited as a **relationship-building target** - develop connection with Flewelling, monitor for future opportunities when circumstances change.
