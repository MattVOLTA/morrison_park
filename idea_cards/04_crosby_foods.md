## Business Units

1. **Eastside Manufacturing Facility** (Rothesay Avenue) -- Molasses processing, packaging, and co-manufacturing of dry powder/liquid blends
2. **Westside Liquid Bulk Terminal** (Port Saint John) -- 51 food-grade storage tanks, third-party liquid storage, now with rail access
3. **Co-Manufacturing Business** -- Contract and private label manufacturing: dry powder blends, dry sugar blends, liquid blends, hot chocolate, drink crystals, gelatin/jelly mixes, creamers, sauces/syrups

## Products

- **Branded:** Crosby's Fancy Molasses, Crosby's Blackstrap Molasses, Crosby's Cooking Molasses, Grandma Molasses (Quebec), Marshmallow Fluff (distribution), drink crystals
- **Contract Manufacturing:** Hot chocolate, powdered beverages (conventional and functional), gelatin/jelly mixes, creamers, sauces/syrups, nutritive sweeteners, private label products
- **Innovation Focus:** Better-for-you products, convenient formats, sustainable packaging
